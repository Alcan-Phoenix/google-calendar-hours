This web app lets you see how many hours you spend on a calendar. It uses the Google Calendar API so you need to authorize the app with the following link:

Connect to Google Calendar

Note: none of the data is saved elsewhere but in your browser. After you close the browser window / tab all fetched data is delete (sessionStorage is used). Thats why you need to reautherize with Google the next time you visit the page. Also the app has read-only access your calendar data.
